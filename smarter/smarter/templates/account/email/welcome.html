Smarter provides unified access to a broad range of large language models from the idustry's best providers including OpenAI, Google AI, Meta AI, Azure Cognitive Services, Amazon and others. Smarter's plugin architecture allows you to easily train models with proprietary data, while keeping your data private. And Smarter's manifest approach to resource management simplifies the process of working with AI resources in the cloud. Smarter's command-line interface (CLI), available for Windows, macOS and Linux, allows you to easily interact with the platform from the command line, as well as from a growing ecosystem of OEM tools.
