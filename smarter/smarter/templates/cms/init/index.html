The Smarter Platform

Built for the evolving needs of generative AI prompt engineers.

Smarter is a no-code platform for securely managing AI resources and private data in the cloud, at scale. It's the smarter way to design, train, test, deploy and monitor generative AI models. Smarter provides unified access to a broad range of large language models from the idustry's best providers including OpenAI, Google, Meta, Azure and others. Smarter's plugin architecture allows you to easily train models with proprietary data, while keeping your data private. And Smarter's manifest approach to resource management simplifies the process of working with AI resources in the cloud. Smarter's CLI, available for Windows, macOS and Linux, allows you to easily interact with the platform from the command line, as well as from a growing ecosystem of OEM tools.

Plugins

Smarter plugins require no coding whatsoever nor data science experience, and they are astonishingly powerful. Turn an ordinary large language model into a domain expert in any subject in a matter of minutes. Plugins are the secret sauce that makes Smarter so powerful. Add as many as you like to your Chatbot, and tune them in real-time. Your private data stays private, and your plugins are yours to keep.

Manifests

Smarter's manifest approach to resource management simplifies the process of working with AI resources in the cloud. A manifest is a human readable form written in YAML that describes your resources. It's a simple way to manage users, models, plugins, chatbots, and more. Manifests are versioned, and can be shared across your team. They can be used to create, update, and delete resources in the cloud.
