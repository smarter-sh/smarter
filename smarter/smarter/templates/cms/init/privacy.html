Privacy

Smarter is committed to protecting your privacy

First and foremost, Smarter is an operating platform for AI resources, not a data broker.

We don't want you to ever be surprised by what happened with your data. Smarter is committed to transparency about how we and our commercial partners collect, use, and disseminate your data. Moreover, anything that might happen to your data beyond its stated purpose will happen only at your explicit direction and prior written approval.

  • We don't sell your data to third parties.
  • We don't use your data to target ads.
  • We don't use your data to train our models.
  • We don't use your data to improve our products & services.

We'll Put It In Writing

We understand that privacy is a big deal. That's why we're committed to putting our privacy policy in writing. Querium Corp., the makers of Smarter, can and will enter into binding legal agreements with your organization to ensure that your data is protected and used only as you direct.

We'll Put Our Gear Wherever You Need

We're familiar with the challenges of data sovereignty and data residency. Cloud infrastructure availability permitting, we can deploy Smarter in the location of your choice. We can also work with you to ensure that your data is stored and processed in compliance with your organization's and/or your nation's data governance policies.

We'll Support Your Federally Mandated Reporting Requirements

We understand that you may have federally mandated reporting requirements. We can work with you to ensure that your data is stored and processed in compliance with your organization's and/or your nation's data governance policies. We can also work with you to ensure that your data is stored and processed in compliance with your organization's and/or your nation's data governance policies. And we are committed to providing you with the certificates and reports you need to demonstrate compliance with your data governance policies.
