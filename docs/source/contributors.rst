Contributors
==============

The Smarter Project is built by a growing community of contributors who believe
that powerful AI tools should be practical, accessible, and open. Whether
you’re fixing a bug, improving documentation, designing new features, or helping
shape the platform’s future, your contributions matter. We’ve invested heavily in
onboarding resources to help you get started quickly, and we welcome participation
from individuals and organizations alike as we build a sustainable ecosystem
around open, community-driven AI infrastructure.

Learn more about contributing to the Smarter Project :doc:`here <smarter-framework/guides/contributing>`.

Core Contributors
------------------

Core Contributors are individuals who have demonstrated sustained commitment to
the project through technical leadership, mentorship, thoughtful collaboration,
and a shared investment in its long-term success. This designation reflects stewardship
rather than seniority or commit volume, recognizing those who help strengthen
both the platform and the community around it.
